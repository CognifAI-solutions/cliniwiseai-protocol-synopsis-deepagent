# Generating Assessments section:

Assessment section contains the following subsections
- Safety Assessments
- Efficacy Assessments

## Generating Safety Assessments

General write up of Safety Assessments. Should be detailed. Use the following example

```
Safety assessment will be carried out as outlined in Section 10.1 of this protocol and adverse event (AE) or Serious Adverse Event (SAE) information will be collected and reported as discussed in Section 13.0 of the protocol. Adverse event and Serious adverse events as and when occur, will be properly recorded, evaluated, managed and reported from signing informed consent till end of treatment assessment visit. Any medical event that occurs after the ICF is signed, will also be recorded and managed as appropriate.

Safety will be evaluated throughout the study based on complete physical examination, including signs and symptoms, adverse event (AE) monitoring, vital signs (blood pressure, pulse rate, respiratory rate and body temperature), Chest X-ray, ECOG status, 12-lead ECG, and laboratory tests. Any clinically significant abnormalities including those present prior to the start of study medication will be recorded in AE section of the eCRF page. Adverse events persisting at the end of the study will be followed up by the Investigator until resolution or until a clinically stable endpoint is reached. All patients who received at least one dose of the investigational product (i.e., the safety population) will be included in the assessments of safety and tolerability.

All AEs will be summarized according to applicable medical coding dictionary.
```
