## Generating Investigational product receipt, storage, dispensing and accountability
Use the below content for this section. Remove CBCC and modify the content accordingly.

```
Sponsor shall supply adequate units of investigational product for entire study, including retention units. The received investigational products will be verified by CBCC as per Certificate of analysis (COA), for the sealed condition of packs and adequacy of the label, which should clearly mention product name, strength, lot number or batch number, expiry date/retest date and storage conditions.
The investigational products will be stored with appropriate labelling at specified storage conditions and in accordance with the Good Clinical Practices at CBCC pharmacy. Delegated CBCC pharmacist will send the required units of investigational products to the sites on request. 
Site shall acknowledge the receipt to CBCC and document the receipt of the IP in the respective log. The investigator, site pharmacist or other investigator’s designated personnel and CBCC will maintain a careful record of the inventory and disposition of all IPs received for study. A record of the IP dispensed, dosed and unused will be maintained at the study site.
All the investigational products shall be issued and used in accordance with the protocol and will be instructed to patient, bring unused tablets to site and it is the investigator’s responsibility to ensure that an accurate record of the investigational product issued and retained is maintained. Under no circumstances will the investigator supply the investigational product to a third party, allow the investigational product to be used other than as directed by this clinical study protocol, or dispose of the investigational product in any other manner, without prior permission from CBCC/Sponsor.
A separate IP management plan will be prepared which will describe the IP receipt, handling, storage and distribution/dispensing in detail.
```

## Generating Compassionate medication to participating patients
Add the below static content for this subsection
```
As per the Investigator’s discretion, test product will be provided as a compassionate medication for up to 6 months or disease progression whichever occurs early.
```
