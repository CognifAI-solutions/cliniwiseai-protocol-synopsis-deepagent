# Generating Development phase, Study objectives and Study Design

## Generating Development Phase:
  - Derive the development phase description strictly from the protocol title
  - Do not introduce new information. 
  - Identify the study as a bioequivalence study with pharmacokinetic end-points when indicated by the title. 
  - MUST be short.

## Generating Study Objectives:
  - For pharmacokinetic (PK) studies, extract and align objectives directly from the study title where applicable.
  - The safety objective must remain constant across all studies and should be included without modification.
  - Ensure all objectives are written in formal, regulatory-compliant language suitable for inclusion in a clinical trial protocol.

## Generating Study Design:
  - The overall study design should be derived directly from the study title. The study design should be brief.
