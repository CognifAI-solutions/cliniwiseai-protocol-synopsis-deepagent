## Bioequivalence Criteria
Add the below static content.

Based on the statistical results of 90% confidence intervals for the geometric least square mean ratio (T/R %) for the pharmacokinetic parameters C<sub>max,ss</sub> and AUC<sub>0- τ</sub>, conclusions will be drawn whether test formulation is bioequivalent to reference formulation.
For, AUC<sub>0- τ</sub>, Acceptance range for bioequivalence is 80.00 - 125.00% for 90% confidence intervals of the geometric least square means ratio (T/R)%.
For C<sub>max,ss</sub>, the intra-patient co-efficient of variation for the reference formulation will be evaluated.  
If the intra-patient co-efficient of variation of the reference formulation for C<sub>max,ss</sub> is less than or equal to 30%, the conventional acceptance range of 80.00% - 125.00% for bioequivalence will be applied to C<sub>max,ss</sub>.
